About Us

NOVA is a clan that was found by its leader Arnold, in a modded client of the Call of Duty franchise which was Modern Warfare 2. Eventually a lot of new players were recruited, and Arnold decided to create a Discord server for the NOVA clan. At first it was a voice/text chat only for the members of the clan, and for Modern Warfare 2 only, but other channels were created for other popular games such as Battlefield 1, PUBG, H1Z1, CSGO etc.. were opened. And that was an initiation for alot of gamers to gather in one place. Now in NOVA Discord servers are counted more than 1.5k gamers and more are going to join. If you haven't joined NOVA Discord's server, then DO it now, just click the button below to join it.
